MTG RAG Test Questions

Basic Rules Tests

1.  If a creature has summoning sickness, can it block?
2.  When exactly do state-based actions check for lethal damage?
3.  What happens if a player attempts to draw from an empty library?
4.  Can a player respond to a mana ability?
5.  When do triggered abilities go on the stack?

Stack & Priority Tests

6.  Player A casts Lightning Bolt targeting Player B. In response,
    Player B casts Healing Salve targeting themselves. What happens?
7.  A player casts Counterspell targeting a spell. In response, the
    controller of the original spell casts another Counterspell
    targeting the first Counterspell. What resolves?
8.  If both players pass priority on an empty stack during the main
    phase, what happens?
9.  A creature with an “enters the battlefield” trigger enters the
    battlefield at the same time as another permanent that affects it.
    Does the trigger still happen?
10. Can a player activate an ability while another spell is resolving?

Replacement Effects & Prevention

11. If a creature would be dealt lethal damage but has an effect that
    says “If this creature would die, exile it instead,” what happens?
12. Two replacement effects both want to modify how damage is dealt. Who
    chooses which applies first?
13. If a player controls an effect that doubles damage and another that
    prevents damage, how is the final damage calculated?
14. If a creature would enter the battlefield with counters but an
    effect says it enters without counters, what happens?

Combat Edge Cases

15. A 2/2 creature with First Strike blocks a 3/3 creature without First
    Strike. What happens?
16. A creature with Double Strike attacks and is blocked by a 2/2. Walk
    through damage step by step.
17. If a creature gains Deathtouch after damage is assigned but before
    damage is dealt, what happens?
18. If all blockers are removed from combat before damage, does the
    attacking creature deal damage?
19. If a creature becomes tapped during combat, does it remain
    attacking?

State-Based Action Stress Tests

20. A creature is a 0/0 but has a static ability giving it +1/+1. Does
    it survive?
21. A player controls two legendary permanents with the same name. What
    happens?
22. A planeswalker has 0 loyalty counters. When is it put into the
    graveyard?
23. A creature has lethal damage marked on it but gains indestructible
    before SBAs check. What happens?

Ambiguity / Judge Call Tests

24. A player says “move to combat” and the opponent says “before combat, I cast…” Is that legal?
25. A spell says “target creature.” The only creature on the battlefield
    has hexproof from that player. Can they cast it?
26. Two triggers controlled by different players trigger at the same
    time. Who chooses the order?
27. A player forgets a mandatory trigger. What happens under the rules?
28. A permanent changes controllers. What happens to Auras attached to it?

Layer System Tests

29. A 2/2 creature gets +2/+2 from one effect and becomes a 1/1 from
    another effect. What is its final power and toughness?
30. A creature becomes a copy of another creature but also has a +1/+1
    counter. What is its final power/toughness?
31. A creature becomes a land. Is it still a creature?
32. A creature loses all abilities but has counters that grant
    abilities. What happens?

Ultra Stress Tests

33. A creature with a replacement effect that modifies how it dies is
    dealt lethal damage at the same time state-based actions are checked
    while another trigger would trigger from it dying. Walk through
    everything in order.
34. Multiple creatures die simultaneously and several “whenever a
    creature dies” triggers happen. How are they ordered?
